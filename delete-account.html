User Data Deletion

Last updated: 29 August 2025

You can delete your account and associated personal data at any time using one of the methods below.

Method A — Email request

Send us an email from your registered address with the subject “Delete my Maris Coin account” and include your user ID (if available).

Email: maris.coin.app@gmail.com

We will confirm your request and delete your account and related data within a reasonable time, except where retention is required by law.

Method B — In-app (recommended)

If you are signed in, you can use the in-app “Delete account” option found under Settings → Account. This action permanently removes your account credentials and associated profile data (subject to legal retention obligations).

What will be deleted?

  • Authentication credentials (email/phone/social login link or wallet ID)
  • User profile document(s) in our database
  • User-owned files in storage (where applicable)

What may be retained?

Minimal logs or records necessary to comply with legal, security, or anti-fraud obligations may be retained as required by law.

Help

If you cannot access your account or the registered email, contact support: maris.coin.app@gmail.com
